  confidence

The score representing how accurately the word was recognized.

@property (nonatomic, readonly) NSInteger confidence

Discussion

While the confidence score can not be individually evaluated on a scale, you can compare the confidences scores between words in a phrase to identify the higher confidence and lower confidence words. This can be useful to provide user feedback, such as underlining the word, for the words that may have useful alternates.
